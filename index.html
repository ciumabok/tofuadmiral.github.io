WatchSafe

WatchSafe challenges the assumption that if a film has inappropriate content, the entire film should be censored. WatchSafe uses computer vision to scan videos for inappropriate content, and blurs only the innappropriate parts for playback using MoviePy. WatchSafe's analytics page illustrates the timestamp, type, and confidence level of innapropriate content.
